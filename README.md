# Sales-Analysis
<h3>Developed a <b>Power BI dashboard</b> to compare and visualize 2023 vs 2024 sales performance.</h3>

<p>
Identified:
<ul>
<li>37.17% growth in total sales (+186.59K)</li>
<li>35.96% increase in orders</li>
<li>14.34% rise in customers</li>
<li>Total units sold increased by 38.08%</li>
</ul>
</p>

<p>Visulaized product category trends as:
<ul>
<li>Clothing as the top-selling segment</li>
<li>Books as the least-selling segment<li>
<li>Electronics as the most growing segment</li>
</ul>
</p>

<p><b>From 2023 to 2024, the sales of electronics increased by 43.5%, toys by 41%, furniture by 40.4%, clothing by 34%, and books by 34%.</b></p>

<p>Highlighted sales regional performance, where California contributing the most by 30.62%, while Illinois contributing the least by 9.41%.</p>
<p>Analyzed the sales tend by month, sales is maximum in June (70K) and the least in July (44K).</p>
