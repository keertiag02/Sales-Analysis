# Sales-Analysis
Developed a Power BI dashboard to compare 2023 vs 2024 sales performance.

Identified a 37.17% growth in total sales (+186.59K), 35.96% increase in orders, and 14.34% rise in customers. Overall, total units sold increased by 38.08%.

Analyzed category trends showing Clothing as the top-selling segment and Books as the least-selling segment, with Electronics as the most growing segment.
From 2023 to 2024, the sales of electronics increased by 43.5%, toys by 41%, furniture by 40.4%, clothing by 34%, and books by 34%. 

Highlighted sales regional performance, where California contributing the most by 30.62%, while Illinois contributing the least by 9.41%. Also analyzed the sales tend by month and identified, sales is maximum in June (70K) and the least in July (44K).
